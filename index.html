b.e. sweeney

is a poet located in the northwest side of chicago, writing on bus rides home about the fear of taking time to love fully. his debut collection, si me quieres besar ("if you want to kiss me"), was published in april 2026, featuring 9 original poems (and two translations into spanish). for information regarding rights, his readings, where to obtain his work, to ask about his choices, or anything at all, reach him at sweeney @ pajamaclaws dot net.
